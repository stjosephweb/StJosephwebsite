About St. Joseph Pastoral Zone

Growing together in faith, prayer and service.

Our Story

St. Joseph was declared a Mission Station on 13 June 2020 and, in just four years, was elevated to the status of a Pastoral Zone on 8 June 2024.

St. Joseph Pastoral Zone is a vibrant and welcoming Catholic community that embraces the diversity of God’s creation and is committed to prayer, evangelization, service, and growth in Christian love.

Our parish family extends beyond the church walls as we bring Christ’s love into our homes, workplaces, and the wider community. We invite you to join us on this journey of faith.

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel, celebrate the Sacraments, foster Christian fellowship, and serve humanity with love and compassion.

Our Vision

To become a Christ-centered parish where every person encounters God, grows in holiness, and contributes joyfully to the mission of the Church.
